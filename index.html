VEEDANA: FEELING, SENATION, EMOTION

Veedana

IS A DJ/EDM PRODUCER
FROM COLOMBIA

Born In Colombia and raised in Florida, John Velasquez better known as Veedana Became interested in EDM at the Young age of 13. During the beginning of his 20s, he joined a promotion company named "Wa$ted Presents" where he met other like-minded people who shared his love for Electronic Music. This is where "VEEDANA" was born. Veedana later went on to open up for big celebrity DJs like Valentino Khan, Deorro, Yellow Claw, 4B, and many others. After he finished his service he went to join Full Sail University as a student to further his knowledge and career in the music industry. there he hopes to graduate and continue on his music journey.
